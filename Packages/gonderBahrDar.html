Cultural Music

Eskista hails from the historic city of Gondar and is native to the Amhara region. Performed by both men and women, usually in groups. Known for its intricate shoulder movements, which are said to be inspired by the sinuous motions of a snake. Dancers create mesmerizing patterns with their shoulders, arms, and upper body. Eskista is an expression of joy, celebration, and community bonding during festivals, weddings, and other special occasions.

Semen Mountain

Simien Mountains National Park is the largest national park in Ethiopia. It lies in the North Gondar Zone of the Amhara Region. The park covers the highest parts of the Simien Mountains, including Ras Dashan, Ethiopia’s highest peak. it is Home to several endangered species, including: Ethiopian wolf, Walia ibex, Gelada baboon,Caracal and etc.

Sekela abay

Sekela Abay is a captivating travel show that embarks on a remarkable journey along the world’s longest river, the Abay (Nile). The show follows the Nile (Abay) for 365 days non-stop. Starting from the source of the Nile in Sekela, the adventure unfolds. it is rich cultural heritage, landscapes, and people along the Nile’s course. From the serene beginnings to the powerful flow downstream, every moment is captured.
